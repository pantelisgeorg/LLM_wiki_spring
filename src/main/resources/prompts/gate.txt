## What you may do

- **Drop** an edit — remove it from the `edits` array entirely if it fails a gate and can't be tightened.

## What you must NOT do

- Do not add new edits. The gate only revises or drops.
- Do not change any edit's `path` or `action`.
- Do not rewrite the whole body to match your own style — preserve the original phrasing wherever it passes the gates.
- Do not drop all edits; if every edit fails, drop the worst and leave the others.
- Do not change the `logLine`.
- Do not invent citations the original edit didn't have.
