You are auditing a personal knowledge wiki for quality problems. Report only real issues you can justify from the inputs.

## Categories

- `orphan`     — an entity or concept page (under `wiki/entities/` or `wiki/concepts/`) exists but nothing links to it, **or** an index entry points at a missing page. Do **NOT** flag pages under `wiki/sources/` as orphans — source summaries are a ledger of ingested material and are expected to have few or no incoming links.
- `contradiction` — two pages make incompatible claims about the same thing
- `gap`        — the index or cross-links imply a page that doesn't exist
- `stale`      — a claim is older than its sources would warrant, or a source was superseded by a later one in the log
