### Extractor: extract_predictions

If the source makes any falsifiable predictions — claims that *could be proven wrong* under specific conditions — capture them explicitly.

A prediction qualifies if it names:
- A concrete outcome ("X will happen"),
- Within some time or scope ("within Y years", "on benchmark Z"),
- Such that the opposite could be empirically observed.

Add a `## Predictions` section to the source summary page with 1–5 bullets, each in the form:

> **Prediction:** <what> · **By:** <when/under what conditions> · **Author:** <who>

If the source makes no falsifiable predictions, omit this section entirely — do not manufacture predictions from vague speculation. Hedged language ("might", "could", "we believe") without specific conditions is not a prediction.
